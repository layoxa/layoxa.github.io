How we work

We partner.
We don't vend.

We're selective about who we build with. The fit is right when you have an existing dental or healthcare network, real conviction in the problem, and clarity on what you bring vs. what you need from us.

We partner, we don't vend.

The best work happens when we're aligned with the operator. Depending on the project, we structure as equity partners, revenue share, or hybrid retainer + equity. Flat-fee builds for spec are not how we operate.

Founder-led engineering.

Every system on this page was architected and built by the same engineer. No junior handoffs, no offshore teams, no sales engineers translating between you and the build.

Healthcare-grade integration depth.

NexHealth, OpenDental, FuzeRx, OpenLoop, Twilio, OpenAI Realtime, ElevenLabs — the plumbing that most AI products skip. We've shipped against all of these in production.
