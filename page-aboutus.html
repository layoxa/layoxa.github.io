Who we are ?

Leyoxa's Mission is to accelerate digital health adoption and provide access to the power of automation without forcing health care providers to adopt to using any specific software. We believe the use of technology should be as natural as how human interact and we are building tools to automate operations of private health sector which will lead to making health care more accessible to people around the world
