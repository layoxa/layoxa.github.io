How we work

Discovery → Build → Ship.

A clinic-first roadmap, secure integrations, and measurable outcomes. We co-build with operators — not for them.

01

Discovery

30-minute call with the engineer. We map the operator's distribution, the patient workflow, and what part of the build is yours vs. ours. No slides — just the problem and the systems already in play.

02

Co-build

We agree on a partnership structure (equity, rev-share, or hybrid retainer + equity), then ship the highest-leverage piece first. Founder-led engineering — no junior handoffs, no offshore teams.

03

Operate

Integration plumbing (NexHealth, OpenDental, FuzeRx, Twilio, OpenAI Realtime, ElevenLabs), production monitoring, and ongoing iteration. We stay in the build — this is a venture, not a contract.
