Leyoxa / FAQ
What did Leyoxa build for DDS Marketing?

Leyoxa partnered with DDS Marketing — a Miami dental agency managing $130M+ in client ad spend — to design and build three integrated AI agents:

  • Voice Agent — picks up every call 24/7, books and reschedules appointments, answers insurance questions, routes to staff when needed.
  • SEO Agent — audits sites, writes location and treatment pages, manages reviews, maintains schema and Core Web Vitals.
  • Marketing Agent — drives retention campaigns, paid ad routing, and growth analytics.

All three agents are running across DDS Marketing's dental client base.

How does Leyoxa structure partnerships?

Depending on the project, we structure as equity partners, revenue share, or hybrid retainer plus equity. Flat-fee builds for spec are not how we operate. The fit is right when you have an existing dental or healthcare network, real conviction in the problem, and clarity on what you bring vs. what you need from us.

Can you integrate with our current PMS or EHR?

Yes. We've shipped against OpenDental, Dentrix, NexHealth, FuzeRx, OpenLoop, Twilio, OpenAI Realtime, and ElevenLabs in production. Healthcare-grade integration plumbing is where most AI products skip — we don't.

Which clinics are a fit for SmileLink?

SmileLink is designed for dental, cosmetic, and multi-provider clinics that want a patient-first mobile experience plus a clean operations dashboard. iOS, Android, and a clinic web dashboard.

How fast can we launch?

Most voice agents launch in weeks. Mobile apps and dashboards follow a phased rollout with early pilot clinics within 6 to 10 weeks.

Is patient data secure and compliant?

We design for HIPAA and PHIPA alignment, using encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, and audit trails across the stack. Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) are available for covered entities upon request.

Who is Leyoxa not the right partner for?

We're not the right partner if you're looking for a flat-fee dev shop, or if you're still validating whether the problem exists. We co-build with operators who already have distribution and conviction — we don't write your business plan.

What does ongoing support look like?

We stay technical end-to-end on the ventures we ship: production monitoring, ongoing iteration, and integration plumbing maintenance. We're co-builders, not contractors. The engineer who built it is the engineer who answers your support emails.

How do I get started?

Book a 30-minute discovery call. We'll map the operator's distribution, the patient workflow, and what part of the build is yours vs. ours. No slides — just the problem and the systems already in play.
